About

Peers was born from the idea of wanting to help colleagues learn better and faster in the overwhelming world of software engineering.
Peers is a platform that allows you to learn and study with peers of the same and different cohorts by creating a study group or joining an existing one.
Peers also gives you the opportunity to meet and network with other software engineers from different cohorts, it allows you to share your knowledge and experience with others and also learn from them.
